English Translation of Legal document: Moss. VII,90.1

Judith Olszowy-Schlanger, Karaite Marriage Contracts from the Cairo Geniza (Boston: Brill, 1997).

Recto

  1. … his [Glory], Highness, Holiness, our Lord and Master Semah
  2. … [?] the guard of a palanquin
  3. … [all those who see] them will recognize them, because they are the seed that the Lord has blessed (Is 61: 9)
  4. … [three] hundred fifty two years of the era
  5. … witnesses at the end, Hizkiyahu, the good young man, may God make him live
  6. … [not] forced, not drunk, not in error, that he betrothed in a qinyan
  7. … sons of Israel, the holy ones. And the mohar that he allocated before his coming
  8. … fifty dinars. The advanced part is twenty dinars
  9. … [of her non-virginity] and if, God forbid, he fails to fulfil this engagement, he shall owe
  10. … [a true], strong and binding debt. And Mevorakh ben Barhōn and Şedaqa ben cElun came
  11. … about herself, in a qinyan, according to her desire and wish
