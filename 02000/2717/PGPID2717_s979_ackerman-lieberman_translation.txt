Ghilāth handed over 150to Tamīm al-Sukkarī, who adde[d]ten thereto; he (Tamīm) will travelwith the total to Upper Egypt.He may buy what he wishes.The profit will be (divided into) halves, exactly,and he receives a surplus (over his partner)by a half a din(ar.)
