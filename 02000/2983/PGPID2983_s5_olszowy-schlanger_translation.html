English Translation of Legal document: T-S 18J5.10a + T-S 10J27.6 + T-S 18J5.10b + T-S AS 115.22

Judith Olszowy-Schlanger, Karaite Marriage Contracts from the Cairo Geniza (Boston: Brill, 1997).

  1. In the name of the [Everlasting] God | who straightens the ways of the pious among the people ... 
  2. And we are [Your] people (Ps. 69:13)... | Let us rise up and build (Neh. 2:18). May the righteous one rejoice in the Lord (Ps. 64: 11) For from …
  3. And I will betroth you to me in | righteousness, justice, compassion and mercy (Hos. 2: 21)... 
  4. Let us begin in the moment of will | from the Supreme God. In the lifetime of our Lord, the great nāsī, David ... 
  5. Eden of the God and during the lifetime of the | fiancé and the fiancée, and during the lifetime of all the community of Israel ... 
  6. like you one thousand times, and he will bless | you as he had spoken to you (Deut. 1: 11). You shall be blessed (Deut. 7: 14)... 
  7. This is the deed of betrothal which took place | on Thursday, which was the day... 
  8. and fifty seven years | of the era of the Greeks, which is the month of Tammuz in the year of difference between [...], in the Royal city which is called …
  9. in this document Khalaf, the young, good | man, may the Rock keep him, ben Maḥfūz, and declared ... 
  10. and mental [faculties], and he is not coerced, | not forced, not drunk and not mistaken... 
  11. ben Manṣūr, to be for him | a wife in purity and holiness, like [daughters of Israel...] | towards ... 
  12. and allocated for her, before he come | [to her,] is fifty coins of silver, mohar of her virginity [...] | her ketubba, and sixty din[ars] 
  13. good, weighed [...] | advanced | As for the forty good delayed dinars, he shall write them for her |
  14. as incumbent upon him in her ketubba. | And if [God forbid] this Khalaf fails to fulfil this engagement, he will owe her half of the advanced payment which is ten 
  15. good, weighed, full dinars | as a true, strong, binding debt. And Barakāt ben Menaḥem ha-Kohen and Yefet ben Abraham ha-Levi came 
  16. and testified about Ama al-Qādar, the young | girl, the virgin, who has full mental faculties, daughter of Efraim, that she called them to witness about herself 
  17. in a qinyan, according to her desire and wish, that she appointed | her father Efraim ben Manṣūr ben Furāt to betroth her to this Khalaf ben Maḥfūz
  18. with the mohar mentioned above, and that she took | upon herself all the conditions that they shall stipulate between themselves. And Efraim ben Manṣūr 
  19. ben Furāt, the father of this young girl, who is her agent, came | and called upon us to witness about himself, in a qinyan, according to his will and wish, that he betrothed Ama
  20. al-Qādar, his daughter, the young girl, the virgin, [to Khalaf] | ben Mahfuz to be his wife and companion according to the custom of the daughters of Israel, 
  21. the pure, faithful and suitable ones, [who behave with their husbands] | in faithfulness, honesty, love, purity and holiness, with the mohar mentioned 
  22. above, in this deed of betrothal. And what [happened before us] | we wrote and signed, to be in the possession of Ama al-Qādar as a proof
  23. and testimony. May they build and prosper, and rejoice in Jeru|salem, the City of God, Zion, the Holy One of Israel. 
  24. And Efraim ben Manṣūr testified about himself that he took [and received the payment of] | the twenty dinars. And Khalaf ben Manṣūr was cleared of them. 
  25. Ṣedaqa ben Masliaḥ ben Sahl ha-Levi (My help is from ...);      Da|vid ben Isaac ben Moses ha-Levi, witness; 
  26. ʿAlan ben Naḥūm, witness; Joseph [ben] ... | ben Efraim, witness;
  27. David [ben Yeshaʿya ?], may he rest in peace; 
  28. … [ben] Naḥūm, witness;
