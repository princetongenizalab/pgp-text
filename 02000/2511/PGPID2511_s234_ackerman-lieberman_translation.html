English Translation of Legal document: Bodl. MS heb. a 3/5

Phillip Ackerman-Lieberman, "A Partnership Culture" (PhD diss., n.p., 2007).

Recto

  1. Proceedings (of what) took place before us—we, the undersigned witnesses—thus:
  2. Mr. Kathīr b. Abraham (who) r(ests in) E(den) came before us, and he had with him a  document from  
  3. our lord Samuel the Nagid, the great Nagid of the people of the Lord of Hosts, prince
  4. of princes,  Nagid of the Nagids, right (hand) of royalty, crown of the Nesi’ut, may his
  5. service be eternal, established forever like the moon. It (the document) demanded the  appearance of the widow of Binyām
  6. b. Ibrahīm (who had come) summoning him to the Glory of the Negidut in the matter
  7. of the orphans of the aforementioned Binyām. Indeed, they only have what he provided  for their needs,
  8. namely, the shops which their father bequeathed them in the town of Malīj, near
  9. Fusṭāṭ Egypt. That which he assessed for their subsistence has not been collected from  their rent.  
  10. Thus the two judges fixed for them a single dinar each month  
  11. for their provisions and clothing . They (the judges) released them from the  declaration
  12. which their father swore, which was under the care of the two courtiers (our) tea(cher)  and m(aster)
  13. Shemariah ha-Kohen, the fullness of beauty, and his partner (our) tea(cher) and m(aster) Samuel b.
  14. 14. (his) h(onor), g(reatness, and) h(oliness, our) tea(cher) and m(aster) Judah, known as  (I)bn Asad, (who) r(ests in) E(den), (an amount) worth twenty  
  15. dinars which will be in the hand of Kathīr, their paternal uncle, who will trade with it  and will add its profit  
  16. to that which will be collected from the rent of the shops, and he will give them a single dinar 
  17. each month. He came and took ten raṭls of silk in our presence
  18. equivalent to the twenty dinars, from the two aforementioned courtiers,
  19. (our) tea(cher) and m(aster) Shemariah ha-Levi and (our) tea(cher) and m(aster)  Samuel. (Our) tea(cher) and m(aster) Kathīr b.  
  20. Abraham said, “Be witnesses for me, performing a qinyan from me, effective  immediately; write and sign
  21. for me with all the appropriate legal formulae and expressions of certainty
  22. and with all the language of claims, that I have received and taken
  23. these twenty dinars, and it has come into my possession and custody.
  24. I have taken upon myself (responsibility for) any danger to it; and I will send to the sons
  25. of my brother Binyām, (who) r(ests in) E(den), the orphans, a dinar each month,  without  
  26. refusal or resistance, nor demanding proof, nor  
  27. defect by means of a defect reducing the payment of this amount.” We performed a  qinyan with  
  28. Mr. Kathīr b. Abraham (who) r(ests in) E(den), a complete and weighty qinyan, with an  implement suitable for doing so  
  29. concerning all that written and detailed above. That which was before us,
  30. we wrote and signed; and that took place on Wednesday  
  31. the twenty-first day of the month of  
  32. Ṭevet which was in the year 146261, in the town of Minyat Ashna,62 which is near  
  33. Fusṭāṭ Egypt, which is situated on the Nile River, the jurisdiction  
  34. of our lord Samuel the great Nagid, Nagid of the people of the Lord of Hosts,
  35. may his service be eternal, established forever like the moon. Proper and clear and  enduring.  
  36. Solomon b. Zer‘a (who) r(ests in) E(den) Peraḥya ha-Kohen b. Aaron (who) r(ests in)  E(den)  
  37. Jachin ha-Kohen b. Saadiah (who) r(ests in) E(den)
