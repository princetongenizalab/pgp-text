English Translation of Legal document: T-S 8J4.21

Phillip Ackerman-Lieberman, "A Partnership Culture: Jewish Economic and Social Life Seen Through the Legal Documents of the Cairo Geniza" (PhD diss., Princeton University, 2007).

Recto

  1. On Thursday, the 27th day of Av, of the year
  2. 1419, in Cairo, M(r.) Eleazar b. Araḥ (who) r(ests in) E(den),
  3. (I)bn al-Shammā‘, and his mother and brother Abraham—his full brother—came before us.
  4. We have already assessed everything in the store at one hundred
  5. and sixty-nine dirhams, and sugar in the house,
  6. and rose marmalade and potions and honey, and its [va]lue—
  7. everything along with the value of the dir(hams) in the store—was in total
  8. eleven dinars and a half.
  9. This M(r.) Eleazar received all that as a bequest of his father, a half-interest of which would be his,
  10. leaving six dina(r)s less a quarter as a financial claim upon him in the agreement
  11. for his brother, the boy, this M(r.) Abraham. The residence //and the slave-girl// remained
  12. and the housewares remained between the two of them. Their mother (is entitled to)
  13. whatever she claims, and whatever his father provided for his mother as well—
  14. he (Eleazar) has no claim therein. We wrote
  15. and signed that which took place before us. Written outside the line: “and the slave-girl”. Enduring.
  16. We performed a qinyan with this M(r.) Eleazar concerning everything therein. Indeed, he has no claim
  17. for that with which he will sustain them. He will be good to them. His brother wished
  18. to leave the six dina(rs) less a quarter with his brother as a debt due him.
  19. Abraham b. Ḥalfōn b. Abraham (may he) r(est) in E(den)   Isaac b. Abraham (who) r(ests in) E(den)
