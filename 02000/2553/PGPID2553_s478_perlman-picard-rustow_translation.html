English Translation of Legal document: Bodl. MS heb. d 66/8

Yaara Perlman, Joshua Picard and Marina Rustow, [digital geniza document edition].

Recto

  1. Testimony of what transpired before us, we the witnesses whose signatures are at the bottom of

  2. this document. Thus it was: Musāfir b. ʿAlī, may his end be good, the ḍāmin of [the sale] and dyeing of silk  

  3. in Damietta appeared before us and said to us: testify concerning me and perform a qinyan with me from this moment, using all the formulations of claims,

  4. [knowing that] I am not forced, nor in error, nor beguiled, but [doing this] of sound heart and volition, that I leased 

  5. all that I have a right to with regard to the ḍamān of silk in Ibwān, with the force of the sublime rescript that I have in my hands,  

  6. may God increase its sublimity and loftiness, to his honor, our teacher and rabbi Yosef the Judge, Felicity of the Yeshiva,  

  7. Man of Israel, son of rabbi Avraham, may his resting place be Eden, and to the two brothers our masters and teachers Daniʾel and Ḥalfon, sons of Yiṣhaq

  8. the ḥazan, may his resting place be Eden, for a period of an entire year, twelve consecutive months, starting with the middle of the month of Shevat

  9. 4866 from the creation of the world

  10. and ending in the middle of the month of Shevat of the year [4]867 from the creation, for the amount of 24

  11. dīnārs, two dīnārs per month. I will take from them each month's installment under the condition that,

  12. whoever makes a claim against them, I will help them against whosoever causes them harm, with all that is in

  13. my power. The three aforementioned elders appeared before us and said to us: perform a qinyan from us from

  14. from this moment using all the formulations of claims that we leased from this Musāfir son of ʿAlī, may his end be good,

  15. everything that was mentioned above. We will pay him for this two dīnārs per month without refusal or  

  16. resistance. We performed a qinyan with each of them and his

  17. fellow with a suitable item, and as a decree

  18. of the Sages. We wrote three copies and gave them [a copy] to serve for each of them

  19. as a right and proof. This was on Monday, which is the eighth day of the month of Shevat 

  20. 4866 from the creation of the world 

  21. in the city of Damietta, which is situated on the great sea and the Nile river. It is valid. 

  22. Avraham … may his end be good, Mevorakh son of ʿAmram
