I


 In Thy Name, 


 the beginning of the reconstruction: 


 Monday, the 11th 


 of Elul: 


 bought 120 ḥimls 


 of clay, paid 36 dir.


 The wages of those who brought it 


 into the synagogue, 3 dir.


 Paid for 5,000 


bricks a sum of 20 dir. 


 The wages of those who brought them in, 2 (dir.). 


 Total, 


 61 dir. 


 Paid for 5 baskets, 3 (dir.). Paid 


 for the meal of the masons and helpers, 5.


 The wages of 5 masons, 29 dir.
