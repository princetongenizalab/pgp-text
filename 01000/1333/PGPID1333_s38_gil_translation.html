English Translation of List or table: T-S K15.45

Moshe Gil, Documents of the Jewish Pious Foundations from the Cairo Geniza (Leiden: Brill, 1976).

Recto

  1. . . . . The old woman, 2 dir. 

  2.  The two rooms of al-Ṣaydalānī, 8 dir. 

  3.  Dār al-Barqī, 5. 

  4.  From Dār al-Ḥaffār: 

  5.  The room of Ḥasan al-Baghdādī, 5 dir. 

  6. The room of Abū Naṣr al-Ḥammāl, 4 dir.

  7.  The room of b. Sulaymān, 6 ½ dir . 

  8.  The room of b. Nehemiah for half a month 2 3/4 dir. 

  9.  The khuṣṣ of Abūʾ1-Ṭayyib, for half a month, nothing.

  10.  The khuṣṣ of Rayyisa's mother, 2 3⁄4 dir. 

  11.  The khuṣṣ of Mukhtār, 2 3⁄4 dir. 

  12.  The khuṣṣ of al-ʿAkkāwi, 2 dir., and he said that he had his lock repaired for 1⁄2 dir. 

  13.  The khuṣṣ of the Kohen's daughter, 2 3/4 dir. 

  14.  The gas of al-Rūmī, 3 dir. (Total,) 269 3/4 dir.

  15.  Also, the rent for Dhūʾl-Ḥijja , collected on time: 

  16.  From Dār Ḥasan al-Ṣabbāgh: 

  17.  The room of Sahlān, 9 dir. 

  18.  The room of Waḥīsh, 5 1⁄2 dir. 

  19.  The qāʿa  a of Umayna, 4 dir. 

  20.  From Dār Quṭayṭ (paid) to Sulaymān b. Zakkār, 12 dir.
