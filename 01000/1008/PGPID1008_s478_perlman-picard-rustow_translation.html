English Translation of Legal document: T-S 13J2.24

Yaara Perlman, Joshua Picard and Marina Rustow, [digital geniza document edition].

Recto

 

1. Proceedings (of what) took place before us—we, the witnesses appointed by the grand Beit Din in the Beit Din of our minister and the leader

2. of our generation, his honor, greatness and holiness, our master and teacher and lord Shlomo the great, sublime minister in Israel and Judah

3. The document worthy of being raised to his attention, may his crown shine in the month of Adar of the year one thousand

4. four hundred and fifty according to the counting of Shṭarot in Fustat of Egypt, situated on the Nile river. Our teacher and Rabbi Aharon 

5. Ha-Kohen al-Iskandarānī al-Ḥarīrī (silk farmer/merchant) son of the elder Moshe, may his resting place be Eden, and Abū l-Khayr Mevorakh son of Moshe, may his resting place be eden, […]

6. the tax-farmer of Aftīḥ, appeared before us with a deed of the gentiles that was in his hand, and whose worth was six dinars. He mentioned that ??

7. in its entirety. Matters, which it would take a long time to explain, happened between them, and after all of that, […] happened

8. […] from the abovementioned. They agreed on three dinars, and this Mr. Aharon Ha-Kohen took it [….]

9. from this deed and from all claim and request from the beginning of time until now, and he received […]

10. dinars with which he was pleased, and it was agreed upon them. We the undersigned made a qinyan f[rom this master and teacher of ours Aharon Ha-Kohen]

11. al-Iskandarānī al-Ḥarīrī son of Mr. Moshe the elder, may his resting place be Eden, to this Abū l-Khayr Mevorakh, a complete and stringent qinyan,

12.  with an implement fitting for the performance of a qinyan from that moment, with his consent and with no coercion at all. He has released him from all claims 

13. and demands, from the beginning of time up till now. Nothing remains between the two of them whatsoever as a joint enterprise (mu‘āmala), and he has no claim against him

14. whatsoever. He deprived him of all oaths, simple and grave, even by way of rider oaths (על ידי גלגול) and even herem

15.  setam. He received the darak from him regarding this , as (this document) is not like promises and not like formularies but rather (bears) the weight and force 

16. of all legal documents of those attentive in the court, so that the force of this document will be exemption. This aforementioned exemption is from the aforementioned deed 

17. and from any other deed that should appear to this Abu l-Khayr Mevorakh

18. in the gentile [script?] or a document in the Hebrew script, and from any daman of silk or other things, and from XXX  that he is aware of

19. now and not aware of now, whatever it might be or amount to. We performed a qinyan concerning from this Mr. Aharon

20. Ha-Kohan, using the most certain stipulations of qinyan, and with the nullification of any determination and pretext, and then we wrote this […]

21. aforementioned so that it would be his right and proof after this day, and everything is proper and enduring. Natan son of Shumuʾel, the friend, may the memory of the righteous be for a blessing […]

22. when Sheikh Abu l-Hasan our teacher and rabbi son of Mr. Yaʿaqov the elder, may his resting place be Eden, who is known as Ibn al-ʿIrāqī, damana to this Mr. Aharon son of Moshe

23. the aforementioned three dinars
