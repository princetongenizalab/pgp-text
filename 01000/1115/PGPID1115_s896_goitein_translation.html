English Translation of Legal document: T-S 13J3.6

S. D. Goitein, Mediterranean People.

Recto (verso as conserved)

  1. A testimony that there appeared before us, we the undersigned witnesses,
  2. the elder Abī l-Riḍā, (his) h(onor), g(reatness and) h(oliness), (our) m(aster and) t(eacher)
  3. Yaʿaqov, the honored elder, the noble and generous, son of (his) h(onor), g(reatness and) h(oliness), (our) m(aster and) t(eacher) Yosef
  4. the elder (may he) r(est in) E(den), and said to us: "I had intended to take over the tax farming (ḍamān) al-Maḥalla al-Kubrā,
  5. which is in the hand of the elder Abī l-Faraj, (our) m(aster) Yeshuʿa, son of R. Avraham, (may he) r(est in) E(den), and of his son
  6. Abī l-Khayr. Then I pondered over the straitened circumstances of these people, their liabilities
  7. and indebtedness to the dīwān and thought that if I took that thing (al-bāb)
  8. from them through machinations (siyāsa), force and getting the better of them, they would suffer damage,
  9. both from the side of (their relation with) the dīwān and their loss of income. Thus, considering their welfare
  10. prior to my own and confident that with God's favorable decree and help the welfare of
  11. all of us would be served, I make this proposal. Of the debts of
  12. these people, I pledge to pay to the dīwān 80
  13. dinars //in the course of four years//, 20 dinars each year, not more than that. Whatever might be above
  14. that sum I am not obliged to pay. This on condition that the
  15. superintendent (mushrif) and director (ʿāmil) grant me the tax farming at the same estimate and price
  16. imposed on them at present without any rise and that
  17. they also relinguish the lease (ḍamān) and transfer it to the aforementioned elder Abī
  18. l-Riḍā (i.e., me) without rise." The aforementioned declared
  19. that the estimate of the lease (ḍamān) imposed on them amounted to
  20. 335 dīnārs. If the superintendent
  21. and director would be prepared to transfer the lease solely with a rise, then
  22. the rise would be included in the sum of 80 dinars mentioned before, and
  23. the elder Abū l-Riḍā would not be obliged to anything more than
  24. 80 dinars for both payment of their debt and a possible rise." On these conditions
  25. the symbolic purchase was made by us from the aforementioned elder Abū l-Riḍā and also
  26. from the aforementioned elder Abū l-Faraj and his son especially on their obligation
  27. to transfer the lease (ḍamān); we made it completely and strictly, valid as from now,
  28. in the prescribed manner, voiding all secret depositions and stipulations. This happened
  29. during the first ten days of the month of Elul, 1458. Correct, valid and confirmed.
  30. (Signed by) Zakkay b. Moshe, (may he) r(est) i(n) E(den). ʿAmram b. R. Yaʿaqov, (may he) r(est) i(n) E(den).
