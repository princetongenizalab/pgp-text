English Translation of Legal document: T-S NS J189

Moshe Gil, Documents of the Jewish Pious Foundations from the Cairo Geniza (Leiden: Brill, 1976).

Recto

  1. (1-2) Al-Shaykh Abūʾl-Faraj b. Maḥfūẓ appeared (in court) and paid in my presence
  2.  
  3. the sum due for removal of garbage to those
  4. who removed it, 30 dir.
  5. waraq. Written by ʿAmrām b. R. Ḥalfōn ha-Levi.
  6. Written by Mevōrākh ha-Levi
  7. b. R. ʿAmrām ha-Levi.
