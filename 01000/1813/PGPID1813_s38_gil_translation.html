English Translation of Legal document: T-S 6J1.1

Moshe Gil, Documents of the Jewish Pious Foundations from the Cairo Geniza (Leiden: Brill, 1976).

Recto

  1. (1-2) We, the witnesses who affix their signatures at the end of this court record, declare that the upper ṭabaqa
  2.  
  3. that is in the compound of the heqdēsh in the Khabīṣa lane,
  4. known as Dār Ibn Bishr, and the ṭārima
  5. that is above the above mentioned ṭabaqa,
  6. are part of its facilities. Herewith we witnessed.
  7. Yeḥīʾēl b. R. Elyāqīm, Nethaneel b. R. Yeshūʿā.
