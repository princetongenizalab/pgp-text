English Translation of State document: ENA 1822a.60

Yusuf Umrethwala, unpublished translations (n.p., 2023).

  1. He is one of the physicians in the hospital of the well-protected Cairo in the neighborhood of [.... He only]
  2. has a small livelihood and barely enough for subsistence. He received from the lofty council, may God perpetuate its authority, a trustworthy promise of a stipend,
  3. through which his spirit was revitalized and his body thrived, namely, that he is to be granted a full five dinars every month. The slave
  4. renews the kissing of the ground and asks for benefactions and kindness towards him with the issuance of the noble decree, may God increase its efficacy. . . .
