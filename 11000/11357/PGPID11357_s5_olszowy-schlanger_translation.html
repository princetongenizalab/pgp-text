English Translation of Legal document: ENA NS 17.24

Judith Olszowy-Schlanger, Karaite Marriage Contracts from the Cairo Geniza (Boston: Brill, 1997).

Recto

  1. And I have betrothed you to me in faith[fulness] (Hos. 2: 21) …
  2. … His hall, and he shall return His Glory to His temple …
  3. … their joy, and He shall fulfil His hidden good news …
  4. … Sivan, of the year one thousand three hundred and four[teen] …
  5. … near Fusṭāt of Egypt which is situated on the river Nile
  6. … may [God] bless him, son of his Glory, Highness, Holiness, our Lord and Master, the elder Daniel ben Bundār before the elders
  7. … from now, and write and sign about me that with full rights I
  8. … [and] not forced, but according to my will, wish and desire of my soul, I have betrothed to me
  9. … for wife, in purity and holiness, according to the custom of Israel, the pure ones
  10. … the mohar of her virginity. And I have added for her one hundred dinars
  11. … dinars, and sixty dinars will remain incumbent upon me and upon my estate after me
  12. … [And if, God] forbid, [this groom] fails to fulfil this matter, she will have half of the mohar
  13. … And his Glory, Holiness, our Lord and Master, the elder Musāfir ben Isaac ha-Kohen and his Glory, Holiness, our Lord and Master Shela ben Joseph came
  14. … who has full mental faculties, and she is fit to fulfil the commandments, daughter of Ḥalfōn
  15. … to betroth her to his Glory, our Lord and Master Ḥalfōn ha-Levi son of his Glory, Holiness, our Lord and Master, the elder, Daniel ben Bundār
  16. … for her, on the conditions mentioned above. And the elder Hillel came
  17. … according to his will and wish that he betrothed Ama al-Wāhid to his Glory, our Lord and Master
  18. … I have paid her the advanced mohar, which is forty dinars …
  19. … their laws, who keep the holy festivals of the Lord …
  20. … and we performed the qinyan with his Glory, our Lord and Master Halfōn, the fiancé, ben Daniel
  21. … what is written and explained above, which is not like asmakhot
  22. … [bind]ing, clear and strong …
  23. … [Ama al-Wāhi]d daughter of his Glory, our Lord and Master Halfōn …
  24. … [the Holy City], may God establish it for [ever] …
  25. … Please, Lord, make us prosper .
  26. … [?] ha-Levi, witness;
