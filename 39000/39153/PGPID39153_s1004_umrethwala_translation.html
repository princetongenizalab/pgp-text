English Translation of State document: UU 934

Yusuf Umrethwala, PGP Translations (n.p., 2023).

  1. In the name of God, the merciful and compassionate
  2. The blessings of God, and his benedictions and best greetings and increased benefactions be upon the imām
  3. al-Ḥākim bi-Amr Allah, commander of the faithful and on his pure ancestors and peace.
  4. The slave of the commander of the faithful, blessings be upon the commander of the faithful, is a man
  5. of righteous background, who oversaw the prisons(?)..and on its people
