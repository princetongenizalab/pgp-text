English Translation of State document: ENA NS 83.262

Yusuf Umrethwala, PGP Translations (n.p., 2023).

  1. The slave of our master and leader [the imām al-Ḥākim bi-amr Allah commander of the faithful]
  2. God’s blessings be upon him [and on his pure ancestors and noble descendants]
  3. [the slave narrates]
