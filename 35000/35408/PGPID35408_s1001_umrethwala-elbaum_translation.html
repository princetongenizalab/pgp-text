English Translation of State document: T-S 8J5.21

Yusuf Umrethwala and Alan Elbaum, PGP Translations (n.p., 2023).

 

  1. The slaves kiss the ground and report that they have a dilapidated synagogue in al-Maḥalla which unites them
  2. in prayer. However, the slaves are [[ being denied]] //afraid of// entering it because it is collapsing.
  3. And if [[this persisted to the extent that]] they would have not approached hi eminence
  4. They have also been prevented from renovating this synagogue. [[the slaves kiss the ground]]
  5. The slaves do not have any rights to exercise over this property except with a decree, from the his eminence, which is forever binding. [or - “they weren't judged to possess the right to engage in rebuilding until (they obtain) a rescript from the his eminence, may he remain powerful"
  6. The slaves ask that benefaction be extended upon them by issuing an order to the qāḍī
  7. ……….restoration of their property as a benefaction
  8. [and] kindness upon.
