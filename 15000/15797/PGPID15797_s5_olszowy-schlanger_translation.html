English Translation of Legal document: ENA NS 7.21

Judith Olszowy-Schlanger, Karaite Marriage Contracts from the Cairo Geniza (Boston: Brill, 1997).

7.21 1 Right Side

  1. and sign about me, that
  2. with full rights
  3. I am declaring
  4. before you. And I am not
  5. coerced, not mistaken, not
  6. in error, not drunk
  7. and not forced, but
  8. according to my will and wish,
  9. with full resolve

7.21 Left Side

  1. and the desire of my soul
  2. that I married, performed the qinyan
  3. and betrothed this
  4. young girl, the virgin,
  5. such-and-such daughter of so-and-so to be
  6. my wife in
  7. purity, holiness,
  8. love and affection,
  9. by mohar, document,

7.21 2 Right Side

  1. witnesses and sexual intercourse,
  2. according to the commandment of Moses
  3. the man of God,
  4. of blessed memory, and according to the custom
  5. of Israel, the pure
  6. and holy ones.
  7. And I will dress,
  8. clothe, support

7.21 2 Left Side

  1. a dignitary,
  2. show him honour, before
  3. the elders who sign
  4. their testimony in this document
  5. and said to
  6. them: Be my
  7. witnesses and perform with me now the qinyan
  8. write
