Transcription of Letter: ENA NS 16.30

Mordechai Akiva Friedman, Jewish Polygyny‎ (in Hebrew) (Tel Aviv: Bialik, 1986).

ENA NS 16, f. 30 ed. Friedman, Jewish Polygyny, pp.262-263 N.H. 04-26-88, (P). Letter to a local dayyan (judge) concerning marital problems of the writer's daughter.
