Recto


The elder Abu'l-Baya'n, (may his) R(ock) p(reserve him), sold through me the small [treatise on the] Art [of medicine] to the elder


Fad'l Alla'h son of the teacher Abu'l-Faraj during the first decade of the month of Tammuz, on the fixed date,


for eleven dirhems. He collected ten and I one dirhem. Solomon wrote this.


The above mentioned elder Abu'l-Bayan, (may his) R(ock) p(reserve him), sold me one Ba'ba' Qa'ma' for which he collected its price


from me - seven dirhems - on the above mentioned date. And peace.


Verso


The elder Abu'l-Bajan sold The Guide through me to the scholarly patron, (may his) R(ock) p(reserve him),


for thirty-two dirhems. Of these he got thirty and I collected two as agent's fees.


That was on Monday, the sixteenth of Tammu'z, 1540, Seleucid Era.


Solomon, son of R. Elijah, (may he come to a) g(ood) e(nd), wrote this.
