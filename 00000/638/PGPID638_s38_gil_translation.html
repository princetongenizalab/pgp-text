English Translation of Legal document: Halper 336

Moshe Gil, Documents of the Jewish Pious Foundations from the Cairo Geniza (Leiden: Brill, 1976).

Recto

  1.  The witnesses named in this deed testify that they know that the compound 

  2.  which is in the blind alley known as the Lane of the Poor in Qaṣr al-Shamʿ 

  3.  that is in Fusṭāṭ Miṣr, namely the compound known as (Dār) al-Hamz, and (also, as Dār al-)Yatīma … 

  4.  They testify …that ⅙ of this compound is a waqf for the two synagogues which …

  5.  . . . for both of them. And as they were asked to confirm the things known by them . . . .

  6.  . . . that they knew, they assented to it, wishing to . . 

  7.  …this deed, on Wednesday, 4 Iyyar . . . . 

  8.  [1]358 of the reckoning to which we are accustomed 

  9.  in Fusṭāṭ Miṣrayim, that is situated on the Nile river, 2 Kislev. 

  10.  Saadya b. R. Ephraim he-Ḥāvēr…

  11.  Joseph b. R. Jacob . Baqā ha-Kohen b. Mukhtār. Yeshūʿā ha-Levi b. R. Solomon.

  12.  This deed was validated before us, in the two courts of the two (synagogues) . . . . 

  13.  . . . (mentioned) above. This validation is included in it at the bottom, in the handwriting of those witnesses who have signed it, namely Saadya 

  14.  b. R. Ephraim he-Ḥāvēr. Joseph b. R. Jacob. Baqā ha-Kohen b. Mukhtār. Yeshūʿā ha-Levi b. R. Solomon, . . . who signed with their own 

  15. (15-16) hand and sign. And since Saadya b. R. Ephraim he-Ḥāvēr and Joseph b. R. Jacob testified before one of the (two) courts, and Baqā ha-Kohen b. Mukhtār and Yeshūʿā ha-Levi b. R. Solomon testified again .. (we, the two) 

  16.  

  17.  courts (accepted) this deed, and validated it accordingly (notes about corrections). 

  18.  Sahlān Segan ha-Yeshīvā b. R. Abraham Beḥīr ha-Yeshīvā.

  19.  Ephraim Zeʿīrā he-Ḥāvēr be-Sanhedrīn Gedōlā, b. R. Shemaryā, of blessed memory.
