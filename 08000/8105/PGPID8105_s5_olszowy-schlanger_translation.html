English Translation of Legal document: T-S 13J37.11

Judith Olszowy-Schlanger, Karaite Marriage Contracts from the Cairo Geniza (Boston: Brill, 1997).

Recto

  1.  … situated on the river Nile, on this day came …
  2. … with full rights, I am declaring before you …
  3. … may he rest in peace, to be my wife in purity and holiness …
  4. … her food, clothes and sexual intercourse to the best of my strength and possibilities, and I shall respect
  5. … who fulfil all their legitimate needs in faithfulness, and honesty. And the mohar
  6. … written […] the fifty coins of silver, which are [the mohar of her virginity] …
  7. … before the elders, witnesses in this writ, and said, we are witnesses …
  8. … of the affairs of her marriage to this Meshullam, the groom, ben …
  9. … her ketubba. And the elder Jacob ben Yeshacya, who is [her agent] came
  10. … with the mohar mentioned above, which is fifty coins of silver, the mohar of
  11. … all that the pure daughters of Israel do in the house of
  12. … the aforementioned […] the advanced mohar, which is ten dinars. And Meshullam was cleared
  13. … five pieces of rock cristal, 2; twelve pieces of silver, a filigree decorated narcissus-shaped [jewel], a jug,
  14. … with a support without two cushions, 30 dinars; two sofas of original Ṭabarī from Ṭabaristān, new, without
  15. … a gilded scarf, 5; a goat-wool veil, 5; a pillow of Dabīqī linen, 5; a Siqlāṭūn kerchief
  16. … ten. The total value of these belongings is of two hundred seventy seven dinars
  17. … like everything which belongs to him, and that he will not change anything without
  18. … [except for what] is worn out. And that this Meshullam, the groom, and […] wanted
  19. … these conditions without
