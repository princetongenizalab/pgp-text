

In the name of God, the merciful and compassionate.


The Jewish slaves report the death of Fakriyya, the Jewess, daughter of 'Abūal-Faraj


ibn Bū Sa'īd, the Jew, leaving by way of heirs having a right


to her inheritance her father, the aforementioned 'Abū al-Faraj, on Wednesday, the


eleventh of the month of Jumādā II, in the year six-hundred and ninety-seven.


Praise be to God, the eternal.
