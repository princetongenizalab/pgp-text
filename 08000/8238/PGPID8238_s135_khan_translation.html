English Translation of Legal document: T-S Ar.39.189

Geoffrey Khan, Arabic Legal and Administrative Documents in the Cambridge Genizah Collections (Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1993).

  1. In the name of God, the merciful and compassionate.

  2. The slaves report the death of 'Alii', daughter of Bū al-'Alā’ ibn Bū Sa'd [on]

  3. Thursday, the twenty-ninth of Dū al-Qa'da, in the year six..hundred and thirty-two.

  4. She left the following heirs: Her aforementioned father, Bū al-'Alā’, and her brothers,

  5. Hiba and Bū Sa'd, the sons of the aforementioned Bū al-'Alā’.

  6. They wrote their signatures in (testimony of) this on the aforementioned date.

  7. Praise be to God, the eternal.
