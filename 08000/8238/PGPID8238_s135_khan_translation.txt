

In the name of God, the merciful and compassionate.


The slaves report the death of 'Alii', daughter of Bū al-'Alā’ ibn Bū Sa'd [on]


Thursday, the twenty-ninth of Dū al-Qa'da, in the year six..hundred and thirty-two.


She left the following heirs: Her aforementioned father, Bū al-'Alā’, and her brothers,


Hiba and Bū Sa'd, the sons of the aforementioned Bū al-'Alā’.


They wrote their signatures in (testimony of) this on the aforementioned date.


Praise be to God, the eternal.
