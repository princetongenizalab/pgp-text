English Translation of Legal document: T-S 16.80

Judith Olszowy-Schlanger, Karaite Marriage Contracts from the Cairo Geniza (Boston: Brill, 1997).

Recto

  1. … a pair of […] dinars …
  2. … and another one; two silver rings, chokers: a pair and a single piece, 15 din.; a pair of earrings, 3;
  3. … a jug, a jewel box, one thousand dirhems; a bridal trunk, 70 din. Clothes: two kerchiefs [?] of Ṣiqlāṭūn,
  4. … and a wrap, 7; a festive dress of Dabīqī linen, 6; dark blue festive dress, 6; a striped cloth lined with silk, 7; a light green festive dress, 15;
  5. … Sicilian, a coat of Dabīqī linen, 5; […] 3; a tunic of Dabīqī linen, a Ṭalī linen undershirt, 5 din.;
  6. … a tunic of Dabīqī linen, 4; […]; two linen shawls, a bathrobe, 4; seven,
  7. … 6; a pair of silk curtains […] Bahnasī, 12; a brocade sofa, brocade rugs,
  8. … red and amber coloured, 30 din.; […] Ṭabarī, 20 din.; a Buzyōn pad, 8; a pad,
  9. … its […], two basins and two ewers [… buckets], 4 toilette baskets, a drinking jug, a Baghdādī bucket,
  10. … [a container] for wax, […] cooking pots, a wash basin, a small bucket, a spittoon, a Damascene cooking pot, a small caldron,
  11. ... 2 cups of khalanj wood, a Muctaşimi serving tray, 5 deep 'china' bowls, 5 Baghdādī fans
  12. … amber coloured […] din.; a pair of chests, 13; a vessel for fragrant wood, 15 din.; and she also brought
  13. [hund]reds din[ars] and a sixth [of a dinar]. The total is one thousand one hundred and seventy
  14. … [that] all these belongings arrived to his house, and that all will remain under his jurisdiction
  15. … [without the knowledge of] his wife Rayyisa and without her consent. He also took upon himself the responsibility
  16. … [and if, God forbid] this Rayyisa leaves this world without a child from him, [all that she brought] will return
  17. … [to her heirs from her paternal family, and this groom will owe] nothing from the delayed [mohar payment]. They also wished and both united themselves in agreement
  18. … [according to the fi]nding of the aviv in the Land of [Israel] …
  19. … we wrote, signed and gave
  20. … [let they re]joice, be merry and …
