English Translation of Legal document: T-S 10J27.12

Oded Zinger, "Women, Gender and Law: Marital Disputes According to Documents of the Cairo Geniza" (PhD diss., n.p., 2014).

Recto

  1. We, the undersigned to this deed, were present 

  2. (2-3) on Sunday, 8 Kislev 1321 to the dating of deeds (27 November 1009) 

  3.  

  4. according to which the date is written in Fustat- Miṣr. 

  5. Sibāʾ bt. Isaac, the dyer, said: “Make the symbolic purchase from me, as of now, 

  6. and write, sign and witness for me that I received 

  7. my bill of divorce from Ḥusayn b. Abraham, my husband. I released him 

  8. from my delayed marriage gift and any trousseau4 that I had in my Ketubba. 

  9. (9-10) No dinar, no dirham, no claim whatsoever, no oath and no ban remain for me with this Ḥusayn. 

  10.  

  11. I have undertaken the maintenance of his child, Harūn (Aaron), his provisions and all his affairs 

  12. (12-13). No claim whatsoever, no harassment, no legal suit nor contention remains for me against him //except his release//. 

  13.  

  14. We, the witnesses, made the symbolic purchase from this Sibāʾ 

  15. bt. Isaac, the dyer, to Ḥusayn, her divorcée, 

  16. b. Abraham regarding everything described in this deed 

  17. with an instrument fitting for purchase. We gave it (i.e. the deed) to 

  18. this Ḥusayn according to the command of Sibāʾ, his divorcée, 

  19. so it will be in his hand a true and established proof. We have also made a symbolic purchase 

  20. from Ḥusayn b. Abraham to Sibāʾ 

  21. bt. Isaac that no movable possession, no claim in the world, no oath and no ban remain for him against Sibāʾ 

  22. //except the correct sending of this deed (?).

Samuel b. Jacob. Mundhir b. Shabbat.
