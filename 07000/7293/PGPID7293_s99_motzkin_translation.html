English Translation of Legal document: T-S 12.620

Motzkin, "The Arabic Correspondence of Judge Elijah and his Family" (PhD diss., n.p., 1965), 2.

Recto

  1. We have examined and testify that [

  2. a qint'a'r g'arawi' and a half of cheese [

  3. of the cheese which was our share [

  4. Yes'u'ah b. Samuel (may he) r(est in) p(aradise)

  5. T'a'ho'r b. Zekhariah (may he come to a) g(ood) e(nd).

  6. Some cheese arrived with Maimo'n b. Nissi'm of Syracuse

  7.                    Its share was sold to the people of the Port City now that

  8. we have established the religiosity of the above mentioned Mr. Maimo'n

  9. and those who have bought it from him: Khalaf and Ibn al-.…

  10. 52 molds, as the witnesses present at the sale testified.

  11. Whoever has been buying any of the cheese may buy it in good

  12. conscience, for it is pure, and sold by a man obeying the laws of purity. We affix our signatures

  13. for the sake of those who have bought (this cheese) and for those who may buy from them, [        ] considering what

  14. (we know) of it, during the last decade of the month of Si'wa'n, of the year

  15. five thousand and one after the creation in No' A'mo'n on the shore of the great sea.

  16. May peace come to all of Israel forever and ever. Isaac b. H'alfo'n

  17. Elljah b. Zekhariah m(ay his) s(oul be) b(ound in the) b(undle of) t(he living).
