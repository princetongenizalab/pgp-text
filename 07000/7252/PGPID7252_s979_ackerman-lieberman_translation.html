English Translation of Legal document: T-S 12.525 + T-S 8J22.16

Phillip Ackerman-Lieberman, "A Partnership Culture: Jewish Economic and Social Life Seen Through the Legal Documents of the Cairo Geniza" (PhD diss., Princeton University, 2007).

Recto

  1. …
  2. […] his [p]artner
  3. […] any court anywhere
  4. [… r]ather the most perfect and certain
  5. […of what] the Sages m(ay their memory be) f(or a blessing) [outlined …] we also have nullified all secret dispositions
  6. and conditions which may be made for us and which we may have made concerning this partnership (shutafut) document, as a decree
  7. of the Sages. We performed a qinyan with this M(r.) Obadiah regarding the payment of the nine dinars
  8. and the partnership (shirka) as well, and with this M(r.) Manasseh ha-Kohen in accordance with (their) will and the appraisal
  9. and the partnership (shirka) as well, with an item suitable for doing so, concerning everything written
  10. and stipulated above. We wrote and signed two copies of this testimony
  11. and gave one (copy) to each of them in order that he would have a title of right and proof.
  12. That took place on the aforementioned date in Fusṭāṭ Egypt, situated on the Nile River.
  13. Proper and enduring. Written on an erasure: “Obadiah”. This is their confirmation:
  14. Ḥalfōn ha-Levi b. Manasseh (who) r(ests in) E(den)     Manasseh ha-Kohen b. Jacob (may his) e(nd be) g(ood)
