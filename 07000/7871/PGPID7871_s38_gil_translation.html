English Translation of Legal document: T-S 10J11.4

Moshe Gil, Documents of the Jewish Pious Foundations from the Cairo Geniza (Leiden: Brill, 1976).

Recto, left side (column a)

  1. Those who affixed their signatures and names

  2. (2-3) at the end (of this document declare) that in the first decade of the month of Teveth of the year 1555

  3.  

  4. of the documents, we were contacted by al-Shaykh

  5. Abūʾl-Thinā al-Ṣayrāfī b. al-Shaykh Abū

  6. Saʿd al-Ḥazzān ha-Qāvuāʿ, of the synagogue of the Jerusalemites,

  7. may it be rebuilt and reestablished during the times of the whole House of Israel,

  8. known as Ibn Ṭayyib, may God preserve him. He requested

  9. us to help him rent

  10. an apartment from the waqf, in the vicinity of the Synagogue

  11. (11-14) where he and his father should establish themselves; but as there was no empty apartment at that time, nor was it possible to evict anybody from his apartment so as to let them receive it, he told us: I am willing

  12.  
  13.  
  14.  

  15. to rebuild the ruined compound, the ruin

  16. known as Dār Sitt Ghazāl, that is adjacent

  17. to the gate of the well belonging to the Synagogue of the Palestinians.

  18. (18-19) You will help me repair it with a small sum, of 200 dir., and also with

  19.  

  20. timber that is at your disposal. I shall spend 

  21. the remaining (sum) for its rebuilding from my own and

  22. rent….

Verso, right side (column b)

  1. A house whose neighborhood is built up is better
  2. (2-3) than one adjacent to ruins. Also there is the fact that it will afterwards yield profit
  3.  
  4. for the entire waqf. So we said to him: Yes,
  5. we will accept your request on the condition that when you
  6. have covered the expenditure that you made
  7. and you decide to live in it (further) you should rent
  8. the apartment from us, for the sum of money
  9. that it will be worth at that time.
  10. He then accepted the condition which we imposed upon him
  11. as mentioned by us above, and we accepted his
  12. request. And we herewith affixed
  13. (13-14) our signatures to this deed as mentioned above and said to him: Adhere to its text
  14.  
  15. concerning our lease so that there be unanimity 
  16. of opinion. (Canceled: greetings). One succeeds
  17. by praying to God. Greetings.
