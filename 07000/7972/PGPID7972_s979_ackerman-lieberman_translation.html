English Translation of Legal document: T-S 13J6.5

Phillip Ackerman-Lieberman, "A Partnership Culture: Jewish Economic and Social Life Seen Through the Legal Documents of the Cairo Geniza" (PhD diss., Princeton University, 2007).

Recto

  1. The statement of the amount of the debt which an acc[ount-book] included
  2. for the group of the partners, which was three hundred [dinars.]
  3. When tw[o of them] separated from the group of the partners,  
  4. being Abū al-Ḥasan b. Abū al-Faraj and Khalaf b. Ḥasan,
  5. the remaining group of the partners were involved with sitting in the storeroom.
  6. For their part of the total from the aforementioned included account-book,
  7. the aforementioned three hundred dinars, which was the capital
  8. allocated to its owners—they are the ones who took it out without
  9. my permission—I, Abū al-Ḥasan b. Abū al-Faraj. From its amount (was)
  10. //from the expenditure of// Abū al-‘Alā b. Abū al-Ṣidād, from Būra, (of which)
  11. the [grou]p of the partners received eighteen dina(rs.) […]
  12. They also received three dinars from him.
  13. The reckoning for Damietta which the account-book included: from Jalāl
  14. al-Dawla, head of the government embroidery workshop, fifty dinars
  15. and from Muntaṣir al-Būrī, twenty-five dinars
  16. and from Fakhr al-Dawla, five hundred dirhams
  17. and from the Coptic clergyman from Barbaya, four hundred dirhams,
  18. and the rest of the amount was divided throughout the town; they took it out
  19. in their hand without my permission. They did not trouble me therein, for
  20. we entered (only periodically) and they were the (regular) keepers of the storeroom.

Right margin

  1. At our separation they guaranteed all that which the account-book allocated us and they paid its total to me,
  2. which entered into our partnership (shirka) ….. a period of six years from when I separated all my business
  3. the same time
