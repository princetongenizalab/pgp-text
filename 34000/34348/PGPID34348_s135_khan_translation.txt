
In the name of God, the merciful and compassionate.

The exalted rescript, may God increase its efficacy, has been issued,
 


dated Muḥarram of the year five-hundred and thirty-two on a petition from the learned judge
 


Sanāʾ al-Mulk ʿAbdallāh ibn ʿAbd al-ʿAzīz ibn Abī Kāmil,
 


in response to his request concerning the restoration of the funds, the oil and the glass that used to be bestowed upon the mosque for which he is responsible out of the warehouse in Fusṭaṭ,
 


(The decree) had the form: Let
 


the appropriate document be issued from the prosperous office.
