English Translation of Letter: ENA NS 18.33

Marina Rustow, [digital geniza document edition].

Recto

  1. [My lord] and [mast]er, my pillar and support, my God perpetuate your existence and prolong your life
  2. [and] elevate your power?* [ . . . ] my love (?). I have already sent a note to his honor my lord
  3. in great haste in which I mentioned to him my arrival. Then he sent letters to me with his messenger, with his well-known
  4. graciousness and attention in everything possible. I thought about what [ . . . ] on his route, and I acted
  5. [i]n a manner that followed from attachment. But success is achieved with God's favor, (and only) then with his. 
  6. The community has showered me with reverence, attention, frequent visits, kindness and hon[o]rs,
  7. in a measure that embarrasses me. I partook of it outwardly, but God knows that inwardly
