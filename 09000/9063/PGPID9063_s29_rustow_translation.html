English Translation of List or table: T-S J1.53

Marina Rustow, [digital geniza document edition].

Recto

  1. An account for my lord the elder Abū Saʿīd Ḥalfon bar Netanʾel, may his resting place be Eden.
  2. To his credit: the value of 8 kawragha and 8 thawb of laundered fabric — 198 dinar and 14 qīrāṭ
  3. To his credit: the value of〚an additional miḥbas (a cloth or garment used as a wrap or pillowcase)〛— 20 kawragha of miḥbas minus 3½ thawb
  4. The value of 4 kawragha and 4 thawb of cloth. Total — 321¼ dinar
  5. To his credit: the value of 6 buhār of pepper, at the rate of 37 dinar per buhār. Value — 222 dinar.
  6. To his credit: the value of 6 buhār and 166 raṭl (pepper) after a discount (Goitein)/weight reduction (Friedman), at the rate of 36 (dinars per buhār). Value — 236 dinar.
  7. To his credit: the value of 10 buhār of pepper from the elder Maḍmūn — dinar.
  8. To his credit: (money) returned from India by the elder Abū Saʿīd —  30 dinar.
  9. To his credit: the value of 31 fūṭa — 15 dinar and 19 qīrāṭ.
  10. To his credit: the value of 20 burd cloaks and an additional black cloak — 30 dinar and 14 qīrāṭ
  11. To his credit: the value of 2 sharābiyya — 3 dinar and 2 qīrāṭ.
  12. To his credit: the value of ⅛ mann of a musk-bag (nāfija) — 4½ dinar.
  13. To his credit: the value of 10 mithqāl of musk particles —  18 dinar. 
  14. To his credit: the value of 8 khamāsiyya of musk —  11 dinar.
  15. To his credit: the value of 6 lifāfa (head-wrap) —  4 dinars and 16 qīrāṭ
  16. To his credit: the value of 2½ dinar-weights of cotton and the value of two scraps (?) —  2 dinar.
  17. Total —  1,496 dinars and 17 qīrāṭs.
