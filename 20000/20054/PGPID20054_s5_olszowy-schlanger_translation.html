English Translation of Legal document: T-S 12.689

Judith Olszowy-Schlanger, Karaite Marriage Contracts from the Cairo Geniza (Boston: Brill, 1997).

Recto

  1. [In the name of] the Everlasting [God]. [God] made him prosper (II Chron. 26: 5)
  2. … Who found etc. (Prov. 18: 22). The steps of a man are directed by the Lord (Ps. 37: 23) …
  3. Etc. The voice of happiness and the voice of joy (Jer. 7: 34) …
