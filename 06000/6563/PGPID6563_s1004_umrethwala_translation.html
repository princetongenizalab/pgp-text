English Translation of Letter: DK 228.3 (alt: 228/B, XIX)

Yusuf Umrethwala, PGP Translations (n.p., 2023).

Recto

  1. …

  2. [I will] send it with whomever you come to an agreement with and I will give him

  3. the price of fodder for two nights, and I told him 

  4. it wasn’t possible for you to send 

  5. today what you had denied - 

  6. to send saddles and reins - for the fear from you

  7. that you will be displeased. And I rented 

  8. a donkey from Qalyūb to 

  9. Shatanof towards the place of

  10.  the //noble// barges and we sped and stayed

  11.  several days [on the barge] and endured on the Nile

  12.  several hardships. When we reached

  13.  Manūf, the Mālik (Malik al-Afḍal)//may God perpetuate his reign// embarked the barge, and embarked 

  14.  with him my master, and my master al-shaykh

  15.  Abū l-Mufaḍḍal and [we] proceeded towards al-Maḥalla. And I stayed 

  16.  on the barge and stayed ….

  17.  the guide. It did not find anyone

  18.  to send a letter with and this

  19.  is the reason for my letters cutting off from you. And when

  20.  my master returned from al-Maḥalla

  21.  I wrote a letter and sent it with 

  22.  Salāma al-Shirjī but it was brief,

  23.  and I wasn’t able to 

  24.  inform him because of the shortage in time. And with regard

  25.  to textiles, which were

  26.  upon me to get it to al-Maḥalla,

  27.  I haven’t done so but 

  28.  have included it along other textiles.

  29.  And I have acquainted myself with everything

  30.  In your letter and have taken cognizance of it. Another letter

  31.  from you also reached us, and I understood

  32.  ………. And deceit with

  33.  al-Shaykh Abū Sulaymān Dāwūd and what 

  34.  You had asked to safeguard…

  35.  It is safeguarded in a trunk.

  36.  And my Master al-Shaykh Abū l-Mufaḍḍal

  37.  May God grant him a long life, I am grateful for his immense benevolence

  38.  May God the Almighty reward him bountifully and not

  39.  deprive me of his grace and make permanent

  40.  his diligence for my requests

  41.  and all of my matters. My regards to you

  42.  and to whomever expands your eminence.

  43.  My regards to your wife.

  44.  And inform her about my immense longing towards her.

  45.  And kiss on my behalf the hand of my master the Rayyis….

Right margin

And regards

Verso

  1. We entered Alexandria and it was []day

  2. And my master wrote explaining

  3. everything and my master lent Abū l-Mufaḍḍal

  4. a mule, and gave me his donkey. And we are aware of

  5. what happened to the Frankish female slave and I have already told you

  6. What is required and I know you have entrusted

  7. Urjuwān (purple cloth)with Abū l-Afrāḥ (ʿArus b. Yosef)

  8. and also garments and all of which you have instructed me

  9. about, I will enact and inform you about….

  10. my matters and regards. 

  11. I wish to inform you about what happened to the wife of al-Shaykh

  12. Abū Saʿīd. Praise be to God for what happened

  13. for the death of the female child, it was inevitable, may God in return give

  14. a male child soon and we congratulate them f

  15. or her delivery and congratulate them on my behalf 

  16. for her delivery and regards.
