English Translation of Legal document: ENA NS 18.37

Judith Olszowy-Schlanger, Karaite Marriage Contracts from the Cairo Geniza (Boston: Brill, 1997).

Recto

  1. to her …
  2. the fat tail, nor the two kidneys and the lobe of the liver nor …
  3. the bread of the Gentiles or their wine or their abominations. And he shall not light the candle on the [Sabbath] evenings …
  4. fire on the Sabbaths evenings, and he shall not make love with her on [Sabbath] days …
  5. [And he shall not force] her to profane the festivals of the Lord of the Ho[sts] …
  6. that he came with her to the religion of the Lord which is the rite of the Karaites …
  7. as it is written: Therefore a man shall leave his father and his mother to cleave unto his wi[fe] (Gen. 2: 24) …
  8. And Şedaqa ben Naḥūm ha-Kohen and Moses ben Sibāᶜa came …
  9. … that she appointed her fa[ther] …
  10. this [?] ben Manṣūr on the conditions they will agree upon …
  11. from him.    And this Ḥasan ben Moses, the father of the girl, came …
  12. in a qinyan, willingly and according to his wish that he betrothed this Karī[ma], the virgin, daughter of …
  13. to be his wife and companion according to the custom of the pure daughters of Israel …
  14. with their husbands in faithfulness, purity and holiness …
  15. mentioned in this deed of betrothal and that he has taken …
  16. this advanced portion from the mohar which is five gold dinars …
  17. ben Manşūr. May they build and prosper, rejoice and be merry …
  18. and be joyous : O Lord save us, O Lord make us prosper …
  19. Yefet ben ᶜEli ha-Kohen ben Zītā, witness;
  20. Isaac ben Jehuda ha-Levi, witness;
  21. Sahlawaya ben Ḥayyim ... ben 'Abdā; David ben Moses; Saᶜid ben …
  22. Efraim ben Mevasser ben Solomon, witness; Isr[ael] is holy and the Lord is for ever; Muᶜammar ben Ṣ[edaqa] …
