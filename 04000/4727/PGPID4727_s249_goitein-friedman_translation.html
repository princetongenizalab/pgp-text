English Translation of Letter: T-S 12.337

S. D. Goitein and Mordechai Akiva Friedman, India traders of the middle ages : documents from the Cairo Geniza : India book (Leiden and Boston: Brill, 2008), vol. 3.

Recto

  1. In Your name, O Merciful.
  2. This is my letter to you, my dear, nob[le] brother and lord [. . .],
  3. may God prolong your life and preserve you and keep you! May he
  4. unite us in the near future (4) in the best of circumstances, ful lling our
  5. happiest hopes in His grace and favor, for He (5) is omnipotent!5

  7. letters, which, however, came into the possession of Mevass r, who did

  11. found me immediately after (I had suffered) a disaster}.7 (9) However, it
