English Translation of Legal document: T-S NS 321.28

S. D. Goitein, "Side Lights on Jewish Education from the Cairo Geniza," in Gratz College Anniversary volume (Philadelphia: Gratz College, 1971), 83-110.

Recto

  1.  
  2. And I received also for the months of Av and Elul together
  3. 12 dirhems  for the three little boys,
  4. namely:  the son of Hilal, the water carrier;  the son of the daughter of Khalaf,
  5. who is the son of the rice-flailer; The son of the daughter of
  6. Miska.Written by Abu ʾ1-Ḥasan, the teacher,
  7. son of Abu ʾ1-Riḍāʾ, the perfumer.
