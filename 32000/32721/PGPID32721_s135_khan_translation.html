English Translation of State document: T-S Misc.22.286

Geoffrey Khan, Arabic Legal and Administrative Documents in the Cambridge Genizah Collections (Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1993).

  1. In the name of God, the merciful and compassionate.

  2. The blessings and benedictions of God, his increasing benefactions and mostexcellent salutation and greetings be

  3. upon our lord and master, the imām al-Ḥāfiẓ li-Dīn Allāh, commander of thefaithful,

  4. and upon his pure ancestors and his most noble sons, with a blessing enduringuntil the day of judgement.

  5. The slave kisses the ground before the lofty, mighty and glorious seat of the

  6. most exalted lord, al-Afḍal, commander of the armies, sword of Islam, thedefender, the protector of the judges

  7. [of the Muslims and guide of the dāʿīs of the believers], may God perpetuate thedominion of him who holds it.
