English Translation of State document: ENA NS 12.15

Yusuf Umrethwala, PGP Translations (n.p., 2023).

Recto

  1. In the name [of God the merciful and compassionate]
  2. Blessings of God [and his benedictions and increased benefactions and greetings be upon our master and leader, the imām]
  3. al-Ẓāhir li-ʾIʿzāz dīn Allah [commander of the faithful, and on his pure ancestors and noble and awaited descendants. The ]
  4. traditions of the exalted presence may God protect its glory
