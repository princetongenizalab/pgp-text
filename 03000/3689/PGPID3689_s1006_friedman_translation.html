English Translation of Legal document: T-S 8J23.23

Mordechai Akiva Friedman, "Divorce Upon The Wife’s Demand as Reflected in Manuscripts from the Cairo Geniza," The Jewish Law Annual 4 (n.p.: Brill, 1981), 103–126.

  1. What does our lord- may his splendour be exalted and his honour increase- say concerning 

  2.  a woman whose husband, a Kohen, has travelled, once, twice,  

  3. and three times. She didn't know the times of his travelling. He  didn't  

  4.  leave her anything to eat or drink.  

  5.  She then swore by oaths that would make the mountains tremble that she  

  6. would not remain with him after this time. Whenever anyone  

  7. speaks to her to reconcile with him, she renews her oaths that  

  8. she won't stay with him.
