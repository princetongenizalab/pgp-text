English Translation of Legal document: T-S 12.449

Judith Olszowy-Schlanger, Karaite Marriage Contracts from the Cairo Geniza (Boston: Brill, 1997).

Recto

  1. …
  2. this ketubba, and on his estate after him …
  3. and her husband shall not owe anything from the [delayed mohar] …
  4. and according to the finding of aviv in the Land of Israel and …
  5. what was before us, we wrote, signed and gave …
  6. and Israel may they build and prosper, rejoice and be merry …
  7. Shela ha-Kohen ben Sadoq beR[abbi] …
  8. Levi ha-Levi bar Joseph, may he rest in peace …
