English Translation of Letter: T-S 13J14.14

Marina Rustow, [digital geniza document edition].

Recto

  1. In your name, merciful one.
  2. May God the exalted listen to what his servant implores his excellency our lord
  3. his honor, greatness and holiness, our master and teacher Mevorakh, sagacious leader, sage of the yeshiva, 
  4. grand counsel, strength of the house of Israel, prince of princes, nagid
  5. of nagids, may his glory be exalted and his honor be magnified, son of his honor, greatness and holiness our master and [teacher]
  6. Saʿadya the physician, pious one of his generation, may the memory of the righteous be a blessing, and may the two honored princes live,
  7. at the head and the beginning, his honor, greatness and holiness our master and teacher the rayyis Abū l-Bayān
  8. Moshe, and may the rayyis Abū l-Barakāt live. The servant has no tongut
  9. to convey supplication and thanks for even some of the benefactions that his excellency has lavished
  10. on his servant. I am sitting among my children, not having ? from God
  11. and from some of his benefactions upon me. My father was validated (had an official appointment) among all the traders 
  12. to request a tax-farm and
