Recto

In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate.
The amount of ḍarības of sugarcane that the warrantors have collected with regard to the plants: 
Fifty-four ḍarības and one sixth.
The monetary value of each ḍarība that they have collected is 35 dīnārs
Giving a total of 1,896 dīnārs.
The amount it yields if the plants are cultivated, from each 
ḍarība, is 5 qinṭārs, a half and a quarter of “red” (raw) sugar,
the price of which is 55 dīnārs, from which there is subtracted the price
of working it by hand, and (for) the press, 5 dīnārs.
