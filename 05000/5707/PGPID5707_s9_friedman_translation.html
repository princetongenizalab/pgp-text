English Translation of Legal document: ENA NS 16.6

Mordechai Akiva Friedman, Jewish Marriage in Palestine: A Cairo Geniza Study (Tel Aviv: Tel Aviv University, Chaim Rosenberg School of Jewish Studies, 1980), vol. 2 (The Ketubba Texts).

  1. [...] May they build and prosper.

  3. [...] of Ḥaṣor which is on the shore of the salty sea. There was wr[itten ...]
  4. [ ... ] b. Japheth (whose) s(oul is at) r(est), said, intentionally, willingly, voluntarily, and of [his own] choice
  5. [... to X daughter of Y] (whose) s(oul is at) r(est), "Be my wife according to the law of the Jews. I will nourish, provide for
  6. [ ... ] and esteem their wives faithfully." And this bride ... consented
  7. [to be married to ... ] the [groom] b. Japheth. And she undertook to honor him, to attend him, and to respect
  8. [him ... -]teen, and two rings, one gold and o[ne] silver, he presented
  9. [ ... ] incumbent upon him ten dinars, the security
  10. [of this marriage contract ... ] Joseph b. ....
