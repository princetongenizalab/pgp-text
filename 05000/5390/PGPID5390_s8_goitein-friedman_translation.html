English Translation of Letter: CUL Or.1080 J92

S. D. Goitein and Mordechai Akiva Friedman, India traders of the middle ages : documents from the Cairo Geniza : India book (Leiden and Boston: Brill, 2008), vol. 1.

  1. [Introductory phrases.] 

  2.  
  3.  

  4. …I have received a letter from Sheikh

  5. Abū Sulaymān Dāʾūd Ibn al-Lebdi, who has also sent a memorandum.

  6. I have now asked God for guidance and sent with Sīdī ʿAyyāsh

  7. a purse containing 100 new dinars, and with Sīdī Iṣḥaq

  8. a purse containing another 100 dinars \\also of {Old} Cairo {Egyptian} currency//. Please, my lord, buy

  9. at your earliest convenience, first: 1 bahār of

  10. pepper 2 bahārs of lac; 1 bahār of costus. Buy me also

  11. 30 ounces of good civet perfume, the best to be had; 4 manns

  12. of ashbāh wood for 12 dinars; 4 manns (13) of ashbāh wood for 16 dinars; 40 manns of bān [...]
