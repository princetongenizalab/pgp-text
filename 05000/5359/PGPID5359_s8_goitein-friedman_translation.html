English Translation of Legal document: T-S 6J2.11

S. D. Goitein and Mordechai Akiva Friedman, India traders of the middle ages : documents from the Cairo Geniza : India book (Leiden and Boston: Brill, 2008), vol. 1.

  1. Testimony given before us, the undersigned [. . . that]

  2. Joseph Lebdi—may God be pleased with him!—son of R. David [before he set out on the voyage],

  3. from which he did not return to Miṣr [made in our]

  4. presence a last will which we have taken down in his name. [He did so while being in full command of his mental faculties,]

  5. without being coerced. We performed with him the symbolic act of obligation[. . .]
