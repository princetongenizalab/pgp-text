  23. This is to inform you, my brother, that
  24. I intend, God willing to come. I have taken out a (passport) {alt. tr.: certificate} other than that of Binyam,
  25. for he too is going up to Fustat {alt. tr.: traveling to Egypt}. I received {alt. tr.: collected} seventeen dinars. I inform you
  26. also that I wrote in Tyre many letters and sent them
  27. with Abraham Ibn al-Qarīṣ and to you as well. Furthermore, I sent with Abraham
  28. of al-Mahdiyya your letter and letters from me, and told him how I was rescued. Thank God
  29. I am well and healthy—may I never miss God’s favor 
  30. under your auspices {alt. tr.: thanks to your auspiciousness}! However, on the day I disembarked, I was so ill
  31. that I did not expect to stay alive, and I wrote those letters
  32. only out of duress. But He acted for the sake of His name, and on the fifth day
