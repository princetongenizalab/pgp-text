recto, right margin

  1. this year some
  2. profitable purchase, as you have
  3. kindly accustomed me to? However, the pepper
  4. this year was cheap and did not return
  5. the capital. It is better not to risk one’s
  6. possessions and go bankrupt {alt. tr.: . . . this year has sold poorly and does not guarantee the capital better than a risky investment on which one loses all}. Please accept, my master,
  7. the best, profuse greetings for your esteemed self. And Peace.
  8. There arrived a letter from your brother
  9. and I am forwarding it to you with this letter.
  10. And Peace. 
  11. I beg to inform you, my master, that the ship
  12. had sailed and could not be seen for some days. Then it came back
  13. safe, thank God.
  14. The aforementioned goods were forwarded to you,
  15. except the almonds, for they were spoiled. I tried to get
  16. others for you; but none arrived in town before the departure
  17. of the people. The iron has arrived.
  18. I took 123 pounds
  19. and Sheikh Khalaf his share,
  20. while Sheikh Maḍmūn took the rest for you.
