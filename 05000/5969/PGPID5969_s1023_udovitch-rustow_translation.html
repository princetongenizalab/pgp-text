English Translation of Letter: T-S 8J25.14

A. L. Udovitch and Marina Rustow, Unpublished editions.

Recto

  1. I am writing, my lord and master — may God prolong your existence and make your glory, support, wellbeing
  2. and happiness eternal — from Alexandria in the middle of Kislev, may God make its blessing known to you.
  3. I would like to inform you that I have written several let­ters to you and have not seen an answer to any of them, I hope due to some happy
  4. distraction. My lord Abū l-Faḍl Zakariyā arrived, but the bales did not arrive with him
  5. but remained behind in Rashīd, may God see to their safety. I inquired of him whether he had any letters from you
  6. but did not find any with him. He informed me that he brought with him a letter to Abū Sḥaq Avraham
  7. b. Faraḥ. I would like you to let me know the news of the silk (shesh) and whether it has been sold. Let me know its unit price
  8. for the dyed variety. I rely only upon God and you for its sale. If my lord
  9. [the elder] Abū Sulaymān b. Duwayda has arrived, please greet him on my behalf. If my master Abū l-Ḥajjāj is present (in Fustat),
  10. give him my regards, and if he is in Būṣīr, convey my greetings to him (there). If you write to him, have him buy me a pair
  11. [of pillows?] for sleeping. He must do this for me. O my brother, I continue to be amazed
  12. [at your aspersions that I] give precedence to the needs of others over your own. What is the negligence you have observed on my part?
  13. This has greatly offended me.

Verso

  1. Please give my best regards to the elder Abu Saʿīd. I have already sent you the information conerning the six garments
  2. [...] that are with my lord Abū l-Faḍl b. Khalaf al-Andalusī and can be found in his bundle of goods. 
  3. [...] Please take delivery of them and try to sell them. Their price was 13 dinars. I am  [. . . ]
  4. in this matter. If there is anything you need, please let me know. Please send [regards] to the children of
  5. [...] and Avraham al-Ṭarābulusī. Peace.

Verso - address

  1. To my lord and master Abū Yaḥyā Nahray b. Nissim,

     

  2. may God lengthen his life and preserve his glory and support.

    1. From the one who loves him, Marduk b. Musa.

    2. May it be delivered to Fustat, God willing.
