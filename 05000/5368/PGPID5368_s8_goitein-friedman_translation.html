English Translation of Legal document: T-S AS 165.190

S. D. Goitein and Mordechai Akiva Friedman, India traders of the middle ages : documents from the Cairo Geniza : India book (Leiden and Boston: Brill, 2008), vol. 1.

  1. This is what happened: [Barakāt, the son of Mr. Joseph]

  2. known as al-Lebdi—may he rest in Eden!—[appeared in court] and said to us: “Be witness to my declaration, write it down

  3. [and hand it over to Mrs. X, the widow of]

  4. the elder Mr. Joseph—may he rest in Eden!, so that it should be in her hand as a legal proof

  5. that I have made this declaration while in good health, etc…

  6. …

  7. …

  8. I have given her a power of attorney over all [. . .]
