English Translation of Letter: ENA NS 48.2

S. D. Goitein and Mordechai Akiva Friedman, India traders of the middle ages : documents from the Cairo Geniza : India book (Leiden and Boston: Brill, 2008), vol. 1.

  1. two days [before] the ships sailed [...]
  2. foreigner by him and b[y…, some of it]
  3. for (or belonging to) the ruler of Dahlak and some of it for Sheikh [...]
  4. Bilāl Ibn Jarīr against the man w[ho sold (?)]
  5. the drky. He bought it from him for [him]self.
  6. And no one can ask for anything claimed by
  7. the lord Bilāl. Were the maṭiyya boats of I[bn]
  8. al-Sudā[n]ī to bring some of it, I would send it to him (i.e., to you).
  9. You should not believe that I have neglected your need
  10. and that which would produce something beneficial to you. By God,
  11. this year there was in the city a great fall (in the market).
