English Translation of Legal document: CUL Or.1080 J58

S. D. Goitein and Mordechai Akiva Friedman, India traders of the middle ages : documents from the Cairo Geniza : India book (Leiden and Boston: Brill, 2008), vol. 1.

 

  1. Date of the bill of divorce:
  2. [Thurs]day, the 27th
  3. day of the month of Nisan,
  4. 1406 E.D.
  5. We, the undersigned witnesses, have checked
  6. this date and found that it was correct,
  7. when we scrutinized it {alt. tr.: have compared . . . upon comparison}. Established and approved.
  8. Jacob b. R. Abraham, may his memory be blessed!
  9. Joseph b. David.
