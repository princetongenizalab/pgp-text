English Translation of Legal document: T-S AS 150.236

Judith Olszowy-Schlanger, Karaite Marriage Contracts from the Cairo Geniza (Boston: Brill, 1997).

Recto

  1. … [In the name] of the Everlasting God … And the name of the Lord is blessed from now [for ever] (Ps. 113: 2).
  2. … suitable for him (Gen. 2: 18). In the Lord [all the seed of Israel] shall be justified and praised (Is. 45: 25) …
  3. … you are for the Lord your God …
  4. … our people. So my heart rejoiced (Ps. 16:9) …
  5. … [for] ever. And I will betroth you to me in faithfulness (Hos. 2: 21) …
  6. … On [Thursday], of the month of Av …
  7. … And he shall zealously defend the holiness of His name …
