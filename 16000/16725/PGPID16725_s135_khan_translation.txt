

In the name of God, the merciful and compassionate,


The slaves report the death of Fāḍila [ ]


the Jew, on Wednesday, the twenty-eighth [in the year]


six-hundred and twenty-[ ] and she left her aforementioned son [ ]


he is called ’Abū Dā'ūd, mentioned herein .. [     on]


the aforementioned date. Praise be to God, the eternal.
