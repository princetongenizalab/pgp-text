English Translation of State document: T-S Ar.38.138

Geoffrey Khan, Arabic Legal and Administrative Documents in the Cambridge Genizah Collections (Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1993).

Recto

  1. In the name of God, the merciful and compassionate.
  2. The benedictions of God and his blessings, his increasing benefactions and most excellent peace and greetings be upon
  3. our master and lord, the imām al-Āmir bi-Aḥkām Allāh, commander of the faithful,
  4. and upon his pure ancestors and most noble sons — enduring benedictions that last 
  5. until the day of judgment. The slave kisses the ground before 
  6. the noble and exalted prophetic presence, may God double its light and raise aloft 
  7. forever its beacon, and reports the continued arrival of the Rūm merchants, who have arrived 
  8. with timber, and whose coming the slave had reported. They are Sergius 
  9. the son of Constantine, .... the son of ... , Grasso the son of Leo the Amalfitan,
  10. ... and Bon Senyon the Genoese, and their companions who are with them.
  11. They say that up to the time of the slave's writing this report, the 
  12. [ ] of the timber that they brought has not been established and no .... has reached them.
