English Translation of Legal document: T-S 6J12.6

Marina Rustow, Unpublished translations.

Recto

  1. I, Natanʾel bar Tamīm, testify that I have guaranteed

  2. for Saʿīd b. Yūsuf the delivery of his signed document, which 

  3. he owes to Mufaḍḍal b. Salāma b. Saʿīd

  4. on account of the signed document that he was supposed to accept from him

  5. in the name of Ibn Najīb al-Dawla

  6. but then he didn’t appear, and indemnifies him from any

  7. obligation, by way of exemption. To that effect, 

  8. Natan ha-Kohen bar Shelomo ha-Kohen wrote his signature.

Right margin

  1. (Also signed) Efrayim b. Yosef.
